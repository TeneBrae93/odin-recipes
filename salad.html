Salad Recipe

Description

This is a fresh garden salad that will be both light and satisfying!

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumbers
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Salad Dressing

Steps

  1. Chop up ingredients to your preferred consistency.
  2. Mix ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Add your choice of salad dressing - french is best!
  4. Mix the salad together with the dressing.
  5. Enjoy the food!!

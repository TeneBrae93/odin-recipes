Burger Recipe

Description

This recipe combines fresh ingredients to make a juicy burger to satisfy your hunger!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Egg
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Lightly oil grill& heat BBQ to medium.
  2. Whisk egg in a bowl& add next 6 ingredients.
  3. Gently shape (don’t firmly press) mixture into burgers about ¾ inch thick.
  4. Using your thumb, make a shallow depression in the centre of each burger to prevent puffing up during cooking.
  5. Place burgers on the grill, close lid& BBQ until NO LONGER PINK INSIDE, turning once, about 6 – 8 minutes per side.
  6. Tuck into a warm crusty bun& add your favourite toppings.
